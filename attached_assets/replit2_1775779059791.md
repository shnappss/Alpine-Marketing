# Replit Prompt: Alpine Marketing — Full Website Build (Hormozi Framework)

## ⚡ THE ONE-SENTENCE BRIEF
Build a single-page, conversion-optimized website for **Alpine Marketing**, a Zurich-based agency that builds full-funnel client acquisition engines for SMBs — from ad creative to closed deal — powered by AI automation, and measured by the only metrics that matter: **CAC and Lifetime Gross Profit (LTGP)**.

---

## STRATEGIC FRAMEWORK: HORMOZI'S VALUE EQUATION APPLIED

Every section of this site is built on Alex Hormozi's Value Equation:

**Value = (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood of Achievement) / (Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice)**

Here's how this maps to Alpine Marketing's website:

| Lever | How We Maximize/Minimize It | Where It Appears |
|-------|---------------------------|-----------------|
| **Dream Outcome** | "A predictable, profitable client acquisition system that runs without you" | Hero headline, Solution section |
| **Perceived Likelihood** | Real case studies with CHF figures, real-time dashboard previews, named tech stack | Case studies, Metrics section, Social proof strips |
| **Time Delay** ↓ | "Results in the first 90 days" / "First response in <60 seconds" | Hero subline, Process section |
| **Effort & Sacrifice** ↓ | "We build it, we run it, you show up to close" / Free audit, no commitment | CTA messaging, How It Works, Guarantee |

**Grand Slam Offer (Hormozi):** The site sells ONE offer: **"The Alpine Growth Engine — a done-for-you, full-funnel acquisition system built in 90 days."** Every section ladders up to this single offer. No service menu. No "pick a package." One engine, one outcome, one CTA.

## SITE STRUCTURE (Hormozi-aligned flow)

The page follows this persuasion sequence, directly derived from Hormozi's landing page framework:

```
1. HERO → Dream Outcome + Proof + CTA (above the fold)
2. PROBLEM → Agitate the pain (the leaky bucket)
3. SOLUTION → Present the full-funnel engine
4. HOW IT WORKS → Show the system (ads → AI → CRM → profit)
5. PROOF → Case studies with real numbers
6. METRICS → The dashboard they'll actually get
7. COMPARISON → AI engine vs. manual (before/after frame)
8. WHY US → Differentiators / risk reversal
9. TEAM → Operators, not theorists
10. CTA → Close with guarantee + urgency
```

Social proof (client logos, stats, or micro-testimonials) appears at **every CTA point** throughout the page — not just in one section.

> **Why We Measure LTGP, Not Just LTV**

>
> We optimize for LTGP because it's the number that tells you which customers are actually profitable — and which ones you should stop chasing.

## COPY TONE GUIDE

### Voice
Confident. Direct. Data-backed. Zero fluff. We're operators who speak in numbers, not buzzwords.

### Perspective
"We" (Alpine) speaking to "you" (the SMB owner or head of sales/marketing).

### Hormozi Influence
- Lead with the **dream outcome**, not the features
- Always pair a number with **context** ("32% CAC reduction in the first 90 days" not just "32%")
- Every claim has **proof** nearby (stat, case study reference, or mechanism explanation)
- Confront the **objection** before they think it ("No, this isn't another chatbot — Claude AI generates context-aware, personalized responses")
- Minimize **perceived effort** at every CTA ("Free. 30 minutes. No commitment.")

### Avoid
- Buzzwords without substance ("synergy," "leverage," "disrupt," "game-changer")
- Generic agency language ("we're passionate about your success")
- Anything that sounds like it was written by a committee
- Jargon without explanation — if you use a term like LTGP, explain it in plain English

### Embrace
- Specific CHF amounts and percentages
- The "leaky bucket" metaphor — use it 2-3 times across the page, not more
- "Systematize" over "optimize" — it signals something bigger
- "Engine" and "machine" — implies it runs without constant attention
- Questions that make the reader audit themselves: "Can you answer what your CAC is right now?"

### Key Phrases

## GLOSSARY (For consistent usage)

- **CAC** = Customer Acquisition Cost. Total spend to acquire one paying customer (ad spend + tools + agency fees ÷ customers acquired).
- **LTGP** = Lifetime Gross Profit. Total revenue from a customer minus the cost of delivering the service. Unlike LTV, LTGP tells you actual profit per customer.
- **LTV/CAC Ratio** = How much customer value you get per franc spent acquiring them. Target: ≥ 3:1.
- **Speed-to-Lead** = Time from lead submission to first response. After 5 minutes, conversion probability drops 21×.
- **Pipeline Velocity** = Speed at which leads move through funnel stages. Faster = shorter sales cycles = lower CAC.
- **Reactivation Rate** = Percentage of dormant/lost leads recovered through re-engagement.
- **TOF / MOF / BOF** = Top / Middle / Bottom of Funnel — distinct ad campaign stages with different creative, targeting, and objectives.
- **GHL** = GoHighLevel, all-in-one CRM for SMBs.
- **n8n** = Open-source workflow automation (Zapier alternative, more flexible at scale).
- **Claude AI Agents** = Anthropic's AI used for lead qualification, intent analysis, and response generation.
